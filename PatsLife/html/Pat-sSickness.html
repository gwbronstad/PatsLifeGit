Pat's Life; a Memoire in Photos

Sickness

This chapter of Pat's life is themed sickness.

At the present stage of medicine, Pat's sickness is inescapable, incurable.

Few pictures correspond to the different markers in her developing illness; often word pictures have to do..

Background of robust good health

2011-06-02

Very much in control of her faculties in 2011, Pat emailed, texted, planned international travel, paid bills and shopped for books and goods on line.

Happy to work with the Microsoft or Apple operating systems, Pat was comfortable with the digital world.

2013-04-21

Pat delighted in her grandchildren.

Hard to know who is having more fun

2014-03-18

Pat relished the grandmother role.

Looks like a product placement for Central Market.

>

2014-04-06

Pat took up running in New Zealand, thanks to Pat Tyler; Pat continued in Portland Oregon and Austin Texas.

It was just for the joy of it..

>

2014-12-30

She took the teaching responsibility to heart. She doubled her pleasure with a glass of wine.

How gratifying this must have been for Patricia.

Stage 1 of illnesses

2016-12-15

Kate is alarmed that Patricia is noticeably changed; loss of interest in books, loss of undersanding of political issues. She had been able to do the N Y Times crossword readily up to Wednesday; but lost interest.

Yet March 17 of 2016, Pat is still reading Alan Cummings "Not My Father's Son".

Both Pat and I were deeply shaken after learning our country had made Trump President. I concluded Pat was in a state of depression that would eventually improve.

2017-03-16

On a drive from Austin TX to Portland OR, Pat as usual insisted on driving "her" Prius. During the drive she grew increasingly confused at changes of highway or road. Pat’s driving became more competence diminished. We returned to Austin by way of Boisie. In the morning, her wit was noticeably diminished. I'd asked her to push on the brake, she replied, “this car doesn’t have a brake.” .

2017-06-20

Pat gets an MRI. In August, her Austin neurologist gives her a diagnosis of Alzheimer's.

2017-10-16

An Austin Neuropsychologist evaluates Patricia; he concurs with the Alzheimer's diagnosis.

2018-04-23

Unhappy with the hopelessness of the Alzheimer's diagnosis, I grasp for the promise of "The Bredesen Protocol" An Austin coincerge practice was willing to support that effort.

It was more an act of despair than act of reason, but we tried for a while.

2018-08-21

We began Central Texas Speech Pathology Services twice a week to try to stem the decline is Patricia's speaking abilities. Of course it is impossible to say whether the rate of decline in speech was lessened or not, but we slowed the utilization of the services as Medicare fund limits approached.

2018-08-27

One of the things Pat most appreciated was to walk around Austin's Town Lake with her friend Pat Johnson.

These ladies have worn out running shoues exercising and enjoying eath other's company along the shores of Austin's Town Lake.
