Onset of Patricia's dementia

Age leaves many and varied marks on us; muscles wane, skin sags, hair betrays us. The unseen symphony of our hormones makes us unwitting puppets of our hidden interior.

If interests and abilities decline, is that not our fate?

2016-12-15

Daughter Kate is alarmed that Patricia is noticeably changed; loss of interest in books, loss of undersanding of political issues. She had been able to do the N Y Times crossword readily up to Wednesday; but lost interest.

Yet March 17 of 2016, Pat is still reading Alan Cummings "Not My Father's Son".

Both Pat and I were deeply shaken after learning our country had made Trump President. I concluded Pat was in a state of depression that would eventually improve.

2017-03-16

On a drive from Austin TX to Portland OR, Pat as usual insisted on driving "her" Prius. During the drive she grew increasingly confused at changes of highway or road. Pat’s driving became less competent. We returned to Austin by way of Boisie. In the morning, her wit was noticeably diminished. In the midst of dealing with heavy frost on the windshield, I'd asked her to push on the brake, she replied, “this car doesn’t have a brake.” .

That jolted me into accepting the seriousness of her illness.

2017-06-20

Pat gets an MRI. In August, her Austin neurologist gives her a diagnosis of Alzheimer's.

2017-10-16

An Austin Neuropsychologist evaluates Patricia; he concurs with the Alzheimer's diagnosis.

2018-04-13

Unhappy with the hopelessness of the Alzheimer's diagnosis, I grasp for the promise of "The Bredesen Protocol" An Austin coincerge practice was willing to support that effort.

It was more an act of despair than act of reason, but we tried for a while.

We began Central Texas Speech Pathology Services twice a week to try to stem the decline is Patricia's speaking abilities. Of course it is impossible to say whether the rate of decline in speech was lessened or not, but we slowed the utilization of the services as Medicare fund limits approached.

2019-01-04

End all Central Texas Speech Pathology appointments; before the month ends, we move to Knoxville, TN

My rationale was: Pat could outlive me, or I could become incapacitated; none of our children remained in Austin. To have some assurance of Pat's care, we moved to Knoxville where Kris and her family lived.

Twice weekly, hour long sessions with speech pathologists were an attempt to arrest the decline in Pat's verbal faculty. Clearly, her speech faculty declined. Whether the rate of decline was slowed or not is impossible to say. At a very minimum it gave the two of us the feeling that we were doing something about an intractible problem. It kept us together at a shared task, and allowed us to go out and about with our lives with a sense of purpose and continuity.

2019-02-04

Moving from Austn to Knoxville meant severing practical connections with Austin physicians. To address Patricia's dementia, she became a patient of The Pat Summit Clinic. For other medical needs, we both became patients at Lakemoor Family Physicians.

2019-06-24

Pat sees imaginagary animals in back yard

The back yard of our rental home in Knoxville had a casual or even wild back yard. There in the jumble of vines, Pat would identify something. Aphasia makes for many ambiguities:

Sometimes Brutus, my daughter's very large dog, is “little B” (the smallish feral cat left behind in Austin.)
Sometimes rabbits and squirrels in the back yard are “Little B”.
Frequently it’s “my cat is there in the back yard, or my cat is dead in the back yard.” Curious about the dimensions of what may be delusion or over-motivated perception, I’ve asked her to guide me to the spot where her dead cat lay.
The effort seemed to dissolve into her awareness that there was no cat where she had perceived one. “Little B” has come to be generic for her need for a pet to pamper. Or perhaps a child-like identification with animals. A step away from rejection of the complexities of the adult world in favor of the simplicity and security of an idealized Winnie-the-Poo world.

2021-01-14

Pat screaming at unspeakable distress.

Pat has pretty much lost the ability to express herself verbally. The neural links between felt need and the ability to give voice to that need, grow more and more frayed. Eventukally only fragments of those esential chains of communication will appear. I was never able to establish alternative code. "Blink once for yes, twice for no." was a skill I could not establish.

Had I practice in training animals, perhaps I could have succeeded. It's also likely that the frayed neural links no longer connected the instructions of another with Patricia's volition.

Indeed in Army basic Training there were a few who could not make the connection between the command: "left Face", and the acto of promptly pivoting to the left.

Patricia's heart crisis

Looking back through this timeline, one can see many forebodings of the crisis. We did consult medical professionals many times, but collectively, we failed to avert the crisis.

Non invasive technologies can peer into our interiors, but the view is still hazy.

2021-02-13

Ambulance to UT Medical Center Emergency

Pericardial effusion, indications of cardiac tamponade, diastolic compression of right ventricle. Surgeon Ben Barton opened chest cavity and removed > 300 ml fluid.

Pre-operative, surgeon Barton warned me that people patricias age were often markedly changed after a surgery of this length.
