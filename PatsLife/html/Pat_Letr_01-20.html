Pat's Letters to Eve Fox

Ordered oldest to newest, in four sets of approxiately 20.

August 20, 1963

Walt had just graduated UT Arlington; armed with an NSF Fellowship Pat and Walt ventured to Ann Arbor where Walt intended to continue an academic career.

June, 1969

Walt was freshly discharged from the Army; he took an early out from the Army for the purpose of going to graduate school. Cutting his enlistment short by two months may have been more important than graduate school. The Viet Nam war had grown more and more objectionable; that Richard M Nixon had ascended to the top of the Chain of Command was simply intolerable. Pat read books while Walt took preparatory classes at the University of Illinois, prior to one more go at Michigan.

Eight

Words can be like X-rays, if you use them properly—they’ll go through anything. You read and you’re pierced.
